The DYCIan Live

A school news broadcasting which aims to establish a dynamic and interactive platform within the school through sharing updates, events, achievements, and relevant information. Students take on various roles such as anchors, reporters, writers, and producers, providing them with valuable hands-on experience in media production and teamwork.

Accomplishments

Coverage of
D’FIRST Fest 2025

In October, The DYClan Live released a special episode featuring the 2025 D’FIRST Fest, filmed from September 30 to October 3. The episode, posted on October 10, captured the fun and creativity of the junior and senior high school celebration.

Aired Live Broadcast

The DYCIan Live broadcast was successfully aired, achieving its goal of engaging the audience and strengthening the DYCIan community. It featured interactive segments, special guests, and smooth execution, with positive audience feedback and growing viewership.

October 23 Live Broadcast

On October 23, the team went live once again to deliver a news episode featuring international, local, and school updates. Topics included Israel’s detention of the Global Sumud Flotilla crew, the discovery of human bones linked to missing sabungeros in Taal Lake, and a “doublet” earthquake in Davao Oriental that affected several communities.

Launching of the
new Opening Billboard

During the posting of the D’FIRST Fest coverage, The DYClan Live proudly unveiled their new Opening Billboard (OBB) in that episode. This marked an exciting update for the program, showcasing a refreshed look and feel that reflects the team’s growth and creativity. The new OBB added a more dynamic and professional touch to the episode, setting the tone for the festive and engaging coverage of the school’s celebration.

for planning

The DYCIan Live Officers, together with their program adviser, held an online meeting to discuss a Plan B in case the live broadcast cannot continue on the scheduled day.
