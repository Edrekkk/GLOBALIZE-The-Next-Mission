GlobaLive

A monthly podcast that explores the DYClan Theme of the Month through thoughtful and engaging conversations. It provides a platform where student hosts and invited guests discuss the theme in depth, offering personal insights, relevant experiences.

Accomplishments

New Horizons
for Globalive

Globalive opened an exciting new chapter by welcoming eight passionate members into its growing circle — including three new hosts ready to bring fresh energy and perspectives. This expansion not only fueled creativity but also strengthened the group’s shared vision of growth, collaboration, and innovation in every production.

Growing the
Globalive Way

With momentum on their side, the team proudly released Episode 2: Growth Month and wrapped up filming for Episode 3: GenTech Month. Each step in the process — from editing to refining the program flow — reflected Globalive’s commitment to improvement and to building a smoother, more dynamic creative system behind the scenes.

One Vision,
One Flow

To keep every idea aligned, Globalive gathered for a key meeting that set clearer directions for the episodes ahead. The result was a seamless rollout — from the release of the GenTech Month episode with Ma’am Sophia Llorente to the filming of Christmas Tree Lighting and two inspiring Green-Centered Month features with Sir Jacques Tigno.

Behind the
Bright Lights

Teamwork came to life as Globalive filmed Christmas Special Episode 2. The production showcased not only the on-screen talent of hosts Julienne Suatengco and Juan Miguelito Garcia but also the artistry of the crew working behind the camera — guided by First Officer Ayesa Figueroa and Program Adviser Ma’am Mikee De Guzman.

Keeping
Globalive Green

In continuation of its advocacy, Episode 4.2: Carrying On the Green-Centered Month Celebration with Sir Jacques took center stage. This episode deepened Globalive’s commitment to environmental awareness, empowering youth and communities to take meaningful steps toward a more sustainable future.

A Season
to Shine

As the year draws closer to its most festive season, Globalive sets the stage for joy and creativity. With November episodes in progress and Christmas Special Episodes on the way, the team pours its heart into crafting content that celebrates unity, purpose, and the spirit that makes Globalive truly special.
